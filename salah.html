Part (i): Finding \(P_3\)

Goal: Hit 3 targets in a straight line in exactly 3 shots.

Example:

Watch the shots:

  1. Shot 1 ➡ Target 1 (Prob \(k\))
  2. Shot 2 ➡ Target 4 (Prob \(k\))
  3. Shot 3 ➡ Target 7 (Prob \(k\))

Total Probability for this specific order:

$$ P = k \times k \times k = k^3 $$
⚽

Part (i): The 8 Possible Lines

That was just one line. There are 8 ways to win:

  • 3 Horizontal Rows
  • 3 Vertical Columns
  • 2 Diagonals

Total = 8 Lines

Part (i): All 48 Cases

For each line, the targets can be hit in any order.

3 Targets = \(3! = 6\) Permutations.

Calculation:

8 Lines \(\times\) 6 Orders = 48 Ways

$$ P_3 = 48 k^3 $$

Part (ii): Finding \(P_4\)

To win in exactly 4 shots:

  • Shot 1, 2, 3: Must contain 2 Hits + 1 Waste.
  • Shot 4: Must be the Winning Hit.

We analyze based on what the "Waste" shot is.

Case 1: The Miss

Scenario: 2 Hits, 1 Miss (Outside), then Win.

Prob of Miss = \( (1-9k) \)

Choose 1 out of 3 targets to be the last one to hit (Win) = \( 3\)

Choose 1 out of 8 lines to be the winning line = \( 8\)

Permutation of 2 Hits and 1 Miss = \( (4-1)! = 3!\)

Watch the ball:

  1. Hit 1
  2. Hit 4
  3. MISS (Outside)
  4. Hit 7 (Win)

Total = \( k^3(1-9k) (3! × 3 × 8) \)
