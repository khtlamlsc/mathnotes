The problem

Cherry plans to make clay bowls and clay cups. The total number of bowls and cups should be at least 22.

Each bowl uses 350 g of clay; each cup uses 200 g. She wants to use at least 5.6 kg of clay.

Costs: $10 per bowl, $8 per cup. Minimize the cost.

Let x be the number of bowls, y be the number of cups (both non-negative integers).
